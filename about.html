About VisaLang

VisaLang is an independent guide that helps people find the right official language exam for visa, residency, citizenship, and work-registration paths. We do not sell exams, we do not book exams for you, and we do not give legal advice.

What we do

We research and organise official language exams — the ones governments, universities, and professional bodies actually accept — so you can identify the right exam before spending money on preparation.

For each exam we link to the official source, describe the CEFR level and format, and give you a checklist of what to verify before booking. Our route finder and cost calculator help you estimate what you need without guesswork.

Coverage

  • 22 guide pages covering Germany A1 through TestDaF and telc B2
  • 50 exam seeds across 6 categories: Language Visa, Medical, Nursing, Interpreter, State License, Healthcare Entry
  • 10 countries in the route finder: Germany, Italy, Netherlands, Portugal, Spain, UK, Finland, Norway, Denmark, France, Canada
  • Every guide includes official source links, Last Updated dates, and a compliance reminder

Official-first approach

Every guide links back to the official exam owner or government source — Goethe-Institut, telc, TestDaF Institute, DUO, Instituto Cervantes, and others. If the source changes, we update the page and refresh the Last Updated date.

We never publish a fixed fee or date without telling you to verify it at the official source. Fees, requirements, and policies change — the official page is always the authoritative reference.

Safe preparation

We describe original practice topics — everyday vocabulary, sample speaking scenarios, study-plan structures — but we never reproduce real exam questions, leaked materials, or anything claiming guaranteed passing. Our advice points you toward official practice materials, not replaces them.

What we do not do

  • Publish leaked questions or copied real exam items
  • Claim authorization from any exam owner or government body
  • Give legal, immigration, or financial advice
  • Sell exams, book exams, or process visa applications

This page is informational only and does not constitute legal advice. Fees, dates, and policies change — confirm with the official source before booking.
