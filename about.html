About VisaLang

VisaLang is an independent guide that helps people find the right official language exam for visa, residency, citizenship, and work-registration paths.

What we do

We compare official exam routes, CEFR levels, fees, test formats, and practice plans. Every route links back to the official exam owner or government source. We do not sell exams and we do not book exams for you.

What we do not do

  • We do not publish leaked questions or copied real exam items.
  • We do not claim authorization from any exam owner.
  • We do not give legal, immigration, or financial advice.

How we work

We research official sources first, then write original practice prompts and planning tools. Every page shows a Last Updated date so you can judge freshness. Before booking, always confirm requirements with the official authority.

This page is informational only and does not constitute legal advice. Fees, dates, and policies change — confirm with the official source.
