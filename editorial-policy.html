Editorial Policy

How VisaLang writes and maintains guides.

Official-first

Every guide links to the official exam owner or government source. We cite what the source says and link to it for verification.

No real exam content

We use original practice prompts only. We do not publish leaked questions, copied real exam items, or anything claiming guaranteed passing.

Freshness

Every page shows a Last Updated date. Fees, dates, and policies change, so we point you to the official source for the current value rather than publishing a fixed number.

Corrections

If an official source changes, we update the page and refresh the Last Updated date. Contact us via the Contact page with the official link.

This page is informational only and does not constitute legal advice. Fees, dates, and policies change — confirm with the official source.
