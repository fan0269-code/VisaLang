Is Goethe A1 always required for Germany family reunion?

No. The exact rule depends on the applicant, family route, exceptions, and authority handling the case. Start with official government or mission guidance, then use VisaLang to organize the exam-prep path.

Can I use VisaLang instead of official sources?

No. VisaLang is an independent planning guide. Always confirm requirements, accepted exams, fees, dates, and documents with the official exam owner or government authority before booking.

Are Goethe A1 fees and test dates fixed?

No. Fees, seats, dates, and local booking rules can change by country and test center. Treat any planning estimate as a prompt to verify the current official booking page.
