## What IRCC confirms

IRCC lists both TEF Canada and TCF Canada for Express Entry and publishes a separate conversion table for each test. Each exam owner controls only its own current format, registration and centre information.

## How to choose

Do not treat raw scores as equivalent across the two tests or assume that both apply to every Canadian programme. First identify the exact IRCC programme, accepted test/version and current conversion table. Then compare the owners' current sessions, centres, fees and formats for the location where you will test.
