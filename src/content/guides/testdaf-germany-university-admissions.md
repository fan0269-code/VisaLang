## What TestDaF is

TestDaF (Test Deutsch als Fremdsprache) tests reading, listening, writing, and speaking at levels equivalent to B2–C1 of the CEFR. It is administered by the TestDaF Institute and is accepted by all German universities as proof of German language proficiency for admission.

        
        
          

## TDN levels and what they mean

TestDaF uses its own scale: TDN 3 (roughly B2), TDN 4 (B2/C1 border), and TDN 5 (C1). Most universities require TDN 4 in all four sections. Some degree programmes accept TDN 3. Always check the exact requirement on the university programme page.

        
        
          

## Before you register

- Confirm the TDN level your target university and programme require.
- Check whether the university also accepts Goethe C1, DSH, or telc C1 Hochschule as alternatives.
- Review the TestDaF exam calendar and registration deadlines for your country.
