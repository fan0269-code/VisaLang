## Exam booking checklist

          
- Passport or accepted ID for exam day.
- Correct exam name: Goethe-Zertifikat A1: Start Deutsch 1 for adults.
- Payment confirmation and appointment confirmation.
- Local centre rules for arrival time, ID, cancellation, and rescheduling.

        
        
          

## Visa file checklist

          

Keep the official certificate, application copies, and any instructions from the German mission together. Requirements can vary by location and case.
