## What it is

CILS B1 Cittadinanza is a B1 Italian exam designed for citizenship applicants. It tests listening, reading, writing and speaking with content focused on everyday Italian life. Administered by the Universita per Stranieri di Siena.

## Who needs it

- Italian citizenship by residence (4+ years for non-EU).
- Citizenship by marriage (2+ years if residing in Italy).
- Naturalisation under the 2018 security decree language requirement.

## What to verify

- Confirm your authority accepts CILS — CELI and PLIDA may also be accepted.
- Check the exam calendar at your nearest Siena centre or authorised partner.
- Record the fee, registration deadline, and retake policy from the centre before paying.
