## Exam structure

telc Deutsch B1 and B2 both have a written exam (reading, language elements, listening, writing) and an oral exam, typically taken on the same day or within a short window. The oral exam is usually conducted in pairs. telc publishes detailed format descriptions and sample materials on its website.

        
        
          

## Preparation approach

- Download the official telc mock exam and format description for your exact exam variant.
- Practise with original speaking topic cards and timed writing tasks that match the telc format.
- Do not use real exam questions or copied test items — safe preparation means original practice prompts.
- If you are preparing for a profession-specific telc (Pflege / Medizin), use the official telc preparation book for that variant.
