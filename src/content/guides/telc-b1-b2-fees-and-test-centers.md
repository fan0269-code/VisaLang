## How to find centres and fees

- Use the telc centre finder on the official telc website and select your country.
- Filter by exam type: telc Deutsch B1, telc Deutsch B2, or specialist variants such as B1·B2 Pflege.
- Contact the centre directly for the current exam fee, available dates, registration deadline, and retake rules.

        
        
          

## Why fees differ

telc does not publish a single global price. Centres set local fees based on location, exam type, oral-examiner costs, and administrative overhead. A centre in one city may charge differently from a centre in another.

        
        
          

## Next action

Use the telc centre finder, shortlist two or three centres near you, compare the fee and date options, then book through the centre — not through a third-party reseller without verifying its status as an authorised telc partner.
