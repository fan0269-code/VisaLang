## Start with the named receiver

The reviewed Sorbonne University Faculty of Arts and Humanities page states B2 French for licence applications and C1 for master's applications. That faculty example only applies to its stated admissions scope. It does not create a rule for every Sorbonne programme, every French university, an employer, or a professional regulator.

## Keep product facts separate from acceptance

France Éducation international identifies DELF B1 and B2 as independent diploma levels and DALF C1 and C2 as separate higher levels. The DELF or DALF diploma itself is valid for life, but that does not establish that a receiver will accept it for every future application or waive its own document and deadline rules.

## Keep other routes outside this page

The reviewed French government worker page does not set DELF B1 or B2 as a universal employment credential. A named employer decides its language requirement, while a regulated profession uses its own competent authority. Do not transfer the Sorbonne faculty example to work or professional registration.
