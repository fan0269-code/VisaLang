## Fee checklist

- Find the Goethe-Institut or authorised exam partner in your country using the official Goethe map.
- Check the local fee for Goethe-Zertifikat B1 (adults), not A1 or C1.
- Confirm payment method, refund policy, rescheduling rule, and registration deadline.
- Budget for one retake unless you are confident of passing on the first attempt.

        
        
          

## Why no fixed price is listed

Fees change by country, centre, and exchange rate. Publishing a stale number creates a false reference. Goethe lists the current local fee on each centre page, and that is the number you should record before booking.

        
        
          

## Next action

Use Goethe's exam-centre finder for your country, record the fee and the date you checked it, then confirm the B1 price with the centre directly before paying.
