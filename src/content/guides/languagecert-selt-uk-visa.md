## What LanguageCert SELT is

UKVI's current SELT list names approved LANGUAGECERT products. UKVI distinguishes routes that require four skills from routes that require speaking and listening, and directs applicants to route-specific guidance for the required CEFR level.

## What approval does not prove

Approval is product-, location- and route-specific. Do not rely on the LANGUAGECERT brand alone. Confirm the route, required level and skills, exact approved product, and approved location on the current UKVI page before booking. Check price, availability and provider terms with LANGUAGECERT after that authority check.

UKVI's reviewed page states that an accepted result must be from an approved test at an approved location and awarded within its stated result-use period.
