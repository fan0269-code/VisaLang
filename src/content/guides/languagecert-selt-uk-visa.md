## What LanguageCert SELT is

UKVI names approved LANGUAGECERT products and distinguishes routes that assess four skills from routes that assess speaking and listening. The route guidance determines the required CEFR level.

## What approval does not prove

Approval is product-, location- and route-specific. Do not rely on the LANGUAGECERT brand alone. Confirm the route, required level and skills, exact approved product, and approved location on the current UKVI page before booking. Check price, availability and provider terms with LANGUAGECERT after that authority check.

GOV.UK states that a result must come from an approved test at an approved location and fall within its stated result-use period. Save that instruction and the provider's current booking terms before paying.
