60-day Goethe B1 study plan for working adults

This is a realistic 8-week plan for someone with a job. It is not a guarantee of passing and does not replace official Goethe practice materials.

Weekly structure

  • Weeks 1–2: Expand vocabulary to work, health, media, and society topics. Daily 20-minute reading drills.
  • Weeks 3–4: Listening practice with German news, podcasts, or radio at B1 speed. Weekly timed writing tasks.
  • Weeks 5–6: Speaking practice with original topic cards. Record yourself and review weak points.
  • Weeks 7–8: Full timed mock sessions (all four modules), weak-area repair, and official format review.

Why this structure works

B1 requires independent language use, not just memorised phrases. A balanced plan that touches all four skills daily — even for 30 minutes — builds the endurance and flexibility the exam expects.

Next action

Before following any plan, compare it with Goethe's current official information, download the official practice materials, and adjust the schedule to your exam date.
