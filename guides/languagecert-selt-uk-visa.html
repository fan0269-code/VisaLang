LanguageCert SELT for UK visa

LanguageCert SELT is the second approved Secure English Language Test provider for UK visas. This page compares it with IELTS UKVI and helps you choose.

What LanguageCert SELT is

LanguageCert offers a Secure English Language Test (SELT) approved by the UK Home Office. It tests reading, writing, listening, and speaking, and is available at the CEFR levels required for UK visa (A1, A2, B1, B2).

LanguageCert vs IELTS UKVI

  • Availability: LanguageCert has fewer test centres than IELTS. Check if there is a centre near you.
  • Format: Both test all four skills. LanguageCert offers computer-based testing at approved centres.
  • Cost: Fees are similar. Compare prices at your nearest centres.
  • Results: Both are accepted by the UK Home Office for visa applications.

How to choose

Both are Home Office approved. Pick the one with a test centre near you and the earliest available date at the CEFR level your visa requires. Do not assume one is easier — both test the same CEFR level.
