Germany family reunion

German family reunion language requirement

Use this page to check the language-requirement question before you book Goethe A1. It is a planning guide, not legal advice: German family reunion conditions can vary by route, authority, and personal situation.

Quick answer

Do not assume A1 is required until you verify your exact route.

BAMF states that family reunification conditions vary depending on whether the person in Germany is a German national, EU citizen, or third-country national. Goethe-Institut states that Goethe-Zertifikat A1: Start Deutsch 1 is recognized as proof of language proficiency for spousal reunification. Put those two facts together carefully: Goethe A1 may be the right proof to check, but the official authority decides whether your case needs it.

What BAMF says

BAMF describes family reunification as a path for spouses and registered partners, parents, and minor unmarried children to migrate to Germany to join family members. It also states that, as a matter of principle, the conditions vary depending on whether you wish to join a German national, an EU citizen, or a third-country national.

That is why a single article should not tell every reader the same answer. Your first task is to identify your route, then verify the language requirement and any possible exception with the responsible German mission or authority.

Common mistakes

  • Assuming every spouse route is identical. Start with your exact family route and responsible authority.
  • Booking from a generic fee page. Fees and dates are local; verify the local booking page before paying.
  • Confusing exam name and level. For adults, Goethe lists Goethe-Zertifikat A1: Start Deutsch 1 at A1 level.
  • Skipping exception checks. If you think an exception may apply, confirm it officially before spending money on exam prep.

FAQ

Does every Germany family reunion applicant need A1 German?

No. Conditions vary by route and authority. Use BAMF to understand the family reunification framework, then confirm your exact case with the German mission or responsible authority.

Where does Goethe A1 fit?

Goethe-Institut states that Goethe-Zertifikat A1: Start Deutsch 1 is recognized as proof of language proficiency for spousal reunification. Check whether your case requires and accepts it before booking.

Can VisaLang tell me whether I qualify for an exception?

No. VisaLang can help you organize the questions, but exceptions and requirements must be confirmed through official guidance or the authority responsible for your case.
