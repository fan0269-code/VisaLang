Goethe B1 speaking topics

The Goethe-Zertifikat B1 speaking (Sprechen) module — the three-part format, a topic bank with sample frames, a paired-interview phrase bank, a self-recording routine, and the most common speaking mistakes. Study aid, not a replacement for official Goethe practice materials.

TL;DR verdict

B1 speaking has three parts: a short self-introduction, a topic presentation, and a paired discussion. The format rewards structure over fluency — a learner who delivers five clean sentences per part usually outperforms one who rambles. Confirm the current part structure, timing, and whether speaking is paired or individual on the official Goethe B1 page.

Who this is for / who should skip

Best for

  • Learners who freeze in the speaking module or run over time.
  • Self-studiers without a speaking partner who need a solo drill routine.
  • Applicants following the 60-day B1 plan who want deeper speaking weeks.

Skip if

  • You already deliver 90 seconds of clean B1 speech without notes — focus on writing instead.
  • You are still at A1 — start with the A1 speaking frame in the 30-day A1 plan.

Topic bank (with sample frames)

Common B1 presentation themes centre on everyday life and opinion. Prepare one five-sentence frame per theme using the template below.

Mein Thema ist ______.
Zuerst möchte ich sagen, dass ______.
Außerdem ist ______ wichtig, weil ______.
Ein Beispiel dafür ist ______.
Zum Schluss finde ich, dass ______.

Recurring themes: a hobby, a recent trip, a course you took, your work, your hometown, a healthy habit, how you learn German, your weekly routine, a festival in your country, your favourite season.

Paired-interview phrase bank

  • Meiner Meinung nach… / Ich bin davon überzeugt, dass…
  • Stimmst du mir zu? / Wie siehst du das?
  • Das ist ein guter Punkt, aber…
  • Kannst du das bitte wiederholen?
  • Ich stimme dir zu / Ich sehe das anders.

Self-recording routine

  1. Record Teil 1 + Teil 2 daily for one week; do not read notes.
  2. Listen back and mark: fillers (ähm), pauses > 3s, grammar slips.
  3. Re-record the parts with the top 3 fixes; compare.
  4. Week 2: add Teil 3 with a partner or by simulating both sides.

Common speaking mistakes & fixes

MistakeFix
Running over time in Teil 2Use the 5-sentence frame; rehearse with a stopwatch.
Over-rehearsed intro; freezes on follow-upPrepare 2 follow-up answers per prompt.
Silent pauses in Teil 3Use fill phrases ("Moment bitte", "Lass mich überlegen").
Defaulting to "du" with examinerDefault to "Sie" unless the format clearly signals informal.

Goethe B1 vs telc B1 speaking

Both test CEFR B1 speaking; Goethe typically uses a short presentation format, telc often leans on paired role-play. Trap patterns (over-running, over-rehearsed intros) are universal. Verify on official pages.
