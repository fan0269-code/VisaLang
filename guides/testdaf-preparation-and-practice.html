TestDaF preparation and practice

This is a preparation guide built around official TestDaF sample materials and original practice routines — no copied real exam questions.

Preparation timeline

  • 3–4 months before: download the official TestDaF sample test, assess your baseline TDN in each section using the official scoring guide.
  • 2–3 months before: daily academic reading and listening practice (university lectures, research abstracts, student-radio segments). Weekly timed writing and speaking drills.
  • 1 month before: full-length timed mock exams using the official sample format. Focus repair on the weakest section.

Resources to use

Start with the official TestDaF model test and the sample answers published by the TestDaF Institute. Supplement with original academic German content — university course descriptions, DAAD articles, and research summaries — not leaked questions or copied real exam items.

Next action

Download the official TestDaF sample test, rate yourself against the TDN scoring grid, and build a weekly schedule from there. Check your target university's TDN requirement before you register for the exam.
