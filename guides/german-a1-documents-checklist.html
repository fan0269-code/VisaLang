German A1 documents checklist

This checklist helps you prepare for booking and visa documentation. It is not a replacement for the official appointment checklist from the German mission.

Exam booking checklist

  • Passport or accepted ID for exam day.
  • Correct exam name: Goethe-Zertifikat A1: Start Deutsch 1 for adults.
  • Payment confirmation and appointment confirmation.
  • Local centre rules for arrival time, ID, cancellation, and rescheduling.

Visa file checklist

Keep the official certificate, application copies, and any instructions from the German mission together. Requirements can vary by location and case.

Official sources

  • Goethe-Institut German examinations
  • BAMF family reunification

Fees, dates, requirements, and accepted exams change — always confirm with the official exam owner or government authority before booking.
