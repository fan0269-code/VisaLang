Goethe A1 speaking topics

This guide gives safe original practice areas for Goethe A1 speaking. It does not reproduce real exam questions.

Safe topic areas

  • Introducing yourself and your family.
  • Shopping, appointments, work, school, and housing.
  • Simple requests, short answers, and everyday routines.

Why these topics fit A1

Goethe describes A1 as basic language knowledge and says the exam includes listening, reading, writing, and speaking sections. Goethe's description of A1 ability includes familiar everyday phrases and simple sentences.

Next action

Practice original one-minute introductions, simple question-answer exchanges, and polite requests. Then compare your preparation with Goethe's official exam information.
