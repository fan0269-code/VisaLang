30-day Goethe A1 study plan

This is a beginner-friendly plan for building A1 routines. It is not a guarantee of passing and it does not replace official Goethe practice materials.

Weekly structure

  • Week 1: alphabet, numbers, greetings, personal information.
  • Week 2: family, shopping, time, appointments, simple forms.
  • Week 3: listening drills, short writing tasks, speaking practice.
  • Week 4: timed review, weak-topic repair, official format check.

Why this structure

Goethe describes A1 as basic language knowledge and says the A1 exam includes listening, reading, writing, and speaking. A balanced plan should touch all four areas.
