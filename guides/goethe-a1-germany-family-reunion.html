Goethe-Zertifikat A1 for Germany family reunion

Use this page to understand why Goethe A1 is a common first check for a spouse or family reunion route to Germany. Do not book from this page alone; always verify the exact requirement with the German mission or authority handling your case.

What to verify first

  • Whether your family reunion route requires proof of basic German.
  • Whether Goethe-Zertifikat A1: Start Deutsch 1 is accepted for your case.
  • Whether any exception applies to your personal situation.

What Goethe confirms

Goethe lists German exams from A1 to C2 and states that Goethe certificates are internationally recognized proof of language knowledge for visa procedures, professional recognition, or university registration. Goethe also states that Goethe-Zertifikat A1: Start Deutsch 1 is recognized as proof of language proficiency for spousal reunification.

Next action

Open the official Goethe exam page, find the nearest exam centre, then separately confirm the visa language rule with the German authority or mission responsible for your application.
