Goethe A1 vs telc A1

Both Goethe and telc are German exam providers. For a visa or family reunion case, the important question is not which brand is famous, but which certificate is accepted for your exact application.

Compare before booking

  • Accepted certificate for your visa route.
  • Nearest test centre and available dates.
  • Exam format, rescheduling rules, and result timeline.
  • Total cost including travel and retake risk.

Goethe-specific note

Goethe states that Goethe-Zertifikat A1: Start Deutsch 1 is recognized as proof of language proficiency for spousal reunification.

Next action

Confirm accepted proof with the German authority or mission handling your file, then choose the provider with the clearest booking path for you.
