Goethe A1 test centers

Goethe states that German exams can be taken in Germany and worldwide at Goethe-Instituts and exam partners. This page helps you check the booking path.

Centre checklist

  • Confirm that the centre offers Goethe-Zertifikat A1: Start Deutsch 1.
  • Check whether the exam is on-site, digital, or has local conditions.
  • Record booking deadline, ID rules, payment method, and result timeline.
  • Keep screenshots or receipts for your personal records.

Common mistake

Do not assume every Goethe location offers every exam on every date. Availability can vary locally.

Official sources

  • Goethe-Institut German examinations

Fees, dates, requirements, and accepted exams change — always confirm with the official exam owner or government authority before booking.
