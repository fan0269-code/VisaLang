VisaLang

Contact

We welcome corrections, official-source updates, and content suggestions. We reply within 2 business days.

Email

Write to us at visalang@visalang.org

For the fastest response, include the page URL you are writing about and the official source link if you are reporting a change.

Report a correction

If you spot outdated fees, dates, or policy information on any guide page, tell us:

  • The exact page URL (e.g. /guides/goethe-a1-fees-by-country.html)
  • The specific sentence or section that needs updating
  • The official source link with the current information

We verify against the official source and update the page within 2 business days. Corrections are always prioritized.

What we can help with

  • Fixing outdated exam information on any guide page
  • Adding a new exam or official source to the library
  • Suggesting a topic for a new guide page
  • Reporting a broken link or technical issue on the site

What we cannot help with

  • Personal visa, immigration, or legal questions — contact the embassy or authority directly
  • Booking an exam or checking your application status — contact the exam centre
  • Translation or interpretation services — we are a guide site, not a service provider

Official sources

Before writing about an exam correction, check the official source first:

  • Goethe-Institut German examinations
  • telc German certificate exams
  • TestDaF Institute

We do not give legal, immigration, or financial advice. Always confirm requirements with the official authority.
