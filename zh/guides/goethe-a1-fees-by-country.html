德国家庭团聚

Goethe A1 各国费用

Goethe A1 pricing is handled locally by exam centres and countries. This page is a fee-check workflow, not a fixed price list.

费用清单

  • 找到你所在国家的 Goethe-Institut 或考试合作方。
  • 核实当地费用对应的是否为 Goethe-Zertifikat A1: Start Deutsch 1。
  • Confirm payment method, refund rule, rescheduling rule, and deadline.
  • 为补考风险和前往考点做好预算。

为什么这里不列固定费用

Fees change by country and exam centre. Publishing a stale number would be worse than telling you to verify the official local page before booking.

下一步行动

Use Goethe's official exam page to locate the appropriate country or centre, then record the fee and Last Updated date in your own checklist.
