Goethe A1 补考政策

Retake rules, waiting periods, fees, and schedules can depend on the exam centre. Treat retake planning as a budget and timeline risk.

要向考点确认的问题

  • 如果没通过，何时可以再次报名？
  • 是否需要再次支付全额费用？
  • 成绩出分有多快？
  • 你的签证预约时间线能否承受一次补考？

为什么重要

A family reunion plan often has document and appointment timing pressure. A retake can affect both cost and timing.
