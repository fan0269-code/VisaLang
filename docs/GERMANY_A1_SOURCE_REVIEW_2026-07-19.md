## Review scope and decision

This review checks only first-party sources owned by the responsible authority, exam owner, or examination centre. It covers:

- `goethe-a1-test-centers`

- `goethe-a1-retake-policy`

  - `/zh/guides/goethe-a1-test-centers/`
  - `/zh/guides/german-a1-exam-booking-timeline/`

Review date: **2026-07-19**.

Overall decision: **SOURCE REVIEW PASS; READER-SPECIFIC LOCAL EXECUTION STILL REQUIRES RECHECK**.

- The three English pages have been reviewed as **fact and verification-process guides**. Their current copy explicitly avoids inventing local prices, dates, seats, refund terms, or certificate timing, so `sourceReviewStatus: reviewed` is supportable for the published claims.
- `reviewed` does **not** mean that a reader's selected centre, date, fee, seat, or booking terms have been closed. Those dynamic details still require a current page or written reply from that exact centre before payment.
- A complete first-party bounded example is available for **China / Beijing / Goethe-Institut Beijing**. Its facts may be published only with that exact centre scope. They must not be presented as China-wide, Shanghai, telc, or worldwide rules.
- The current Goethe rules establish that Start Deutsch 1 is a non-modular examination: after an unsuccessful result, the examination is repeated as a whole. The reviewed retake copy must preserve that distinction.
- First-party Chinese pages support source review of the selected facts. A separate Chinese wording review must compare the final rendered copy, not merely reuse the English status or infer translation quality from the existence of an official Chinese source.

## Facts that cannot be safely closed without a selected centre

- whether that centre is currently listed or authorised by the exam owner;
- whether it currently offers the adult `Goethe-Zertifikat A1: Start Deutsch 1` or the intended telc A1 product;
- current exam dates, registration opening, deadlines, and seats;
- the exact fee and eligibility for any learner discount;
- payment method, invoice/receipt rules, and payment deadline;

- whether a missed or failed attempt requires a new full-price registration;
- any centre-set retake period and the next available seat;
- ID, name-matching, test-day, result-release, certificate-issue, collection, and delivery procedures;
- whether the responsible German mission accepts the exact proof for the applicant's route and circumstances.

The Beijing sources close these items only where they state an answer for Goethe-Institut Beijing. They do not close Shanghai, another Chinese partner, telc in China, or a centre outside China.
