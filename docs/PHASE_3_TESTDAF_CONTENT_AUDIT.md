## Non-negotiable editorial guardrails

- The target university and specific programme decide accepted language proof, minimum score, application materials, and admission conditions.
- An official exam provider explains its examination; it does not replace a university or programme admissions decision.
- The selected test centre decides its local fee, dates, identification, cancellation or change terms, result process, and certificate process.
- The competent local German mission decides its local visa materials and procedure.
- If a claim has no verifiable, current, in-scope official source, the guide may give only a reader-side verification action, not a conclusion.
- Do not use "usually", "most", "typical", or "common" as substitutes for the actual university, programme, centre, or local-mission rule.
- Do not state fixed fees, seats, registration deadlines, result or certificate timing, visa results, admission results, language exemptions, or cross-university acceptance guarantees.
