# Phase 2 Germany B1 source-review record

Review date: 2026-07-19

Scope: eight Germany B1 core-route guides, their explicit next/supporting route, and the B1 settlement/citizenship Hub.

Review role: `source-review`
Outcome: the final public claims and verification workflow on all eight core pages were reviewed; reader-specific route, locality, proof acceptance, exception, booking, fee, date and submission decisions remain with the named authority or selected centre.

This record supersedes the 2026-07-13 editorial `Finalized` label. That historical label was not a controlled `sourceReviewStatus` and was not used to promote any page.

## Review rule and authority hierarchy

1. Current consolidated German statute controls legal terminology and route-specific conditions.
2. The competent local foreigners authority or nationality authority controls the individual route, accepted evidence, exceptions and procedure.
3. Federal Government and BAMF pages provide bounded orientation or test context; they do not replace a local decision.
4. Goethe-Institut, telc and BAMF define their exam products.
5. The selected official or authorised centre controls current local dates, seats, fees, identification, cancellation, accessibility, results and certificate processes.

`reviewed` means that the final page's published facts, source scope, boundaries and reader verification actions were checked. It does not mean that a reader's case or dynamic local information was verified.
