## Editorial guardrails for follow-up work

- Use the source record above only within its stated scope. If an official page changes scope or cannot be opened, retain a manual official-source verification step rather than substituting a third-party source.
- Do not add fixed fees, dates, result times, availability, accepted-certificate guarantees, exemption conclusions, or individual-case advice without a current, exact, in-scope official source.
- Preserve the reader journey: requirement -> accepted proof -> official centre -> fee and booking terms -> timeline and documents -> preparation or retake.
